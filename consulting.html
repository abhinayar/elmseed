Consulting

One-on-one personalized consulting services

Elmseed consultants work with clients to enhance entrepreneurial knowledge and to build on the skills necessary for business growth and success. Whether it’s learning to pitch your business to potential funders, keep track of your finances, or develop a business plan, our consultants are ready and excited to work with you. All entrepreneurs, from those just starting up to those with established businesses, are encouraged to work with our consultants.

As a client, you will be assigned personal Elmseed consultants who are available to meet with you based on your schedule. Our consultants work to develop close working relationships with their clients, and clients are encouraged to contact consultants by phone or email whenever a business question or problem arises. Our client relationships are extremely important to us, and they are at the heart of Elmseed’s social mission.

Elmseed consultants work with clients on a variety of projects. Our consultants are equipped to help you create financial projections, build plans for your business, and conduct market research, just to name a few. No two Elmseed clients are the same, and our individualized consulting model allows us to tailor our approach to fit your needs. The more information you provide us, the better we can serve you and your business.
