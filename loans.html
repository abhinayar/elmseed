Microloans

Loans to help take your business to the next level

Elmseed recognizes that each business is unique in its needs and objectives, and thus demands unique attention in its solutions and strategies. To best serve those in need of capital to make business ideas a reality, Elmseed offers microloans to promising entrepreneurs through its Finance department. The Elmseed microloan helps small business owners to finance their entrepreneurial goals.

What are the terms of the loan?

Elmseed offers loans of up to $5,000 at a 10% annual interest rate. For example, a $2000 loan repaid over the course of 12 months will have a monthly loan repayment amount of $176. However, this interest rate can be dropped to the federal minimum interest rate (usually less than 1%) in a given month if you meet with an Elmseed consultant or finance officer to discuss the status of your business.

How can I increase my chances of a successful loan application?

Loan applicants with the most success are those with a strong understanding of business operations and goals, organized financial records, and detailed plans for the funds. Most microloans disbursed through Elmseed are given to clients who have an established relationship with the organization, either through our Entrepreneurial Development Course or through consulting. Elmseed values the relationships we establish with our clients, and we believe that these relationships can be a good indicator of client dedication and reliability.

What required documents should I collect prior to applying for an Elmseed loan?

1. A full business plan
2.Basic financial projections
3. Proof of income
4. Last fiscal year’s tax returns
5. Copies of all pertinent licenses to be submitted with the application

What happens if I am missing a required document?

If you are missing either of the first two required documents, our consulting office is more than happy to work with you on this endeavor. Enrollment in Elmseed’s consulting program is highly encouraged and can take place concurrently with the loan application process.

What does the process look like? How long will it take to get a loan decision?

After setting up an initial meeting, a loan officer will work with you to review business financials and assess loan feasibility. If eligible, the loan officer will assist you in underwriting your loan application. The loan application is then submitted to our Board of Directors for review and a final decision. Although varying from client to client, this is often a 1-2 month process.
