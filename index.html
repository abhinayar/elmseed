About Us

The Elmseed Enterprise Fund’s mission is to create successful businesses in New Haven by providing start-up capital, business training, technical assistance, and a network of peer support. Elmseed is the oldest student run micro-finance firm in the nation and is staffed by a dedicated team of Yale undergraduate students.
